# How to Export Reports from LiveIQ (Subway)

Follow these steps to download any SubwayIQ report as a CSV and save it to your device.

---

## Steps

1. **Open LiveIQ** on your device.

2. **Tap the three lines (☰) in the top-left corner** to open the menu.

3. **Navigate to SubwayIQ Reports** — it's listed under the **Reports** section in the menu.  
   You'll land on a page showing all available reports (see list below).

4. **Click the report you want to download.**

5. **Click Export**, then select **CSV**.

6. The file is now downloaded to your device.
